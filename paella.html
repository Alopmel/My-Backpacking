PAELLA

The paella, 1 or also to the paella, 2 (from the Valencian paella, which means frying pan and refers to the container with which the recipe is made and which gives it its name, and this in turn from the Latin patella) 3 It is a recipe based on rice, originating in the current Valencian Community (Spain), nowadays very popular throughout Spain and also in Argentina 4. In this recipe the rice is cooked together with other foods in a frying pan, generally wide and with handles.
